    🌳 How to play
    
    
    Find the hidden target opening. Each guess is another chess opening, and your clue is how many opening moves it shares with the target before the line branches away.
    

    
    Your goal
    
    
    Guess the exact opening within 15 guesses. A normal guess costs 1. A hint reveals the next target move on the tree and board, and costs 3 guesses.
    

    
    How clues work
    
    
    After each guess, the game compares its move sequence with the target and keeps the deepest shared prefix. If your guess makes progress, the board plays the newly confirmed moves in order.
    
    
    Target:  1.e4 e5  2.Nf3 Nc6  3.Bb5      (Ruy Lopez)
Guess:   1.e4 e5  2.Nf3 Nc6  3.Bc4      (Italian Game)

→  shared through 2...Nc6, then your line splits off.
    

    
    Reading the tree
    
    
      
      
    • Blue moves are confirmed to be on the target's path.
      • 
      
    • Red moves are where one of your guesses branched away.
      • 
      
    • The tip of the blue trunk is the deepest point you've confirmed.
      • 
      
    • The full answer appears in gold ★ only when you win, give up, or run out of guesses.
      • 
    
    
    
    
       on target path

    The board shows the position after the deepest confirmed line. It never jumps ahead of what the tree has confirmed.
    

    
    Difficulty
    
    
    Each day has four puzzles: Easy / Medium / Hard / Expert. Easy uses famous main openings; Expert reaches deeper and more obscure theory.
    

    
    Search by name (najdorf), ECO code (B90), or moves (1. e4 e5 Nf3). Daily puzzles are the same for everyone; Practice mode gives endless random puzzles with separate stats.
