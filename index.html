    ♟ How to play

    Guess the hidden chess opening. Each guess is another opening, and the tree shows how far its move order matches the answer before it branches away.

    How to read it

    • Blue moves are confirmed on the answer path.
    • Gray moves are wrong branches from your guesses.
    • Gold ★ appears after you guess the opening's name.
    • Wrong guesses can share their own branches, revealing how those openings relate.
    • The board shows your deepest confirmed position; select a blue move to replay an earlier one.

    Guesses and hints

    • Search by opening name or ECO code. Turn on Search using notation in the top bar to search by move order.
    • Hints reveal one move and cost 3 guesses.
    • Easy has 10 guesses, Medium 15, Hard 20, Expert 25.
