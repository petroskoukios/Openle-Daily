    🌳 How to play
    
    
    A hidden target opening is chosen each day. Guess openings to discover it.
       Your only clue is how far you travel down the same branches of the opening tree before your line splits away from the target's.
    

    
    The clue: the opening tree
    
    
    For every guess we find the deepest shared sequence of moves with the target. Your guesses grow a tree, and where each one branches away tells you how close it was.
    
    
    Target:  1.e4 e5  2.Nf3 Nc6  3.Bb5      (Ruy Lopez)
Guess:   1.e4 e5  2.Nf3 Nc6  3.Bc4      (Italian Game)

→  shared through 2...Nc6, then your line splits off.
    
    
    The deeper your guesses run with the target, the warmer you are. You have 15 guesses; each hint reveals one more target move and costs 3 guesses. When a guess's whole line matches the target, you win.

    Difficulty
    
    
    Each day has four puzzles — Easy / Medium / Hard / Expert — and you can play any of them. Easy is the famous main openings; Expert reaches deep into obscure theory.
    

    
    Search by name (najdorf), ECO code (B90), or moves (1. e4 e5 Nf3). Daily puzzles are the same for everyone; Practice mode is endless random openings with its own stats.
