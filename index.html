    How to play

    Guess the hidden chess opening. Each guess branches where its move order stops matching the answer.

    Read the tree

    • Blue moves are confirmed on the answer path.
    • Gray moves belong to diverged guesses.
    • ★The star appears after you guess the opening's name.
    • ⑂Related wrong guesses can share their own branches.
    • ▦The board shows your deepest confirmed position. Select any move to replay it.
